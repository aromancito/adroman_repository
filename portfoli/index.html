SOME PROJECTS...

Turning ideas into impactful experiences. Explore the magic within each project in my portfolio.

MARIO KART RACE CIRCUIT

During my university journey, I delved into the realm of 3D Graphics in one of my modules. As a Mario Kart enthusiast, I chose to channel that passion into my project. I crafted my own circuit and replicated essential in-game objects. It was my way of seamlessly blending my love for video games with my academic pursuits. How does that sound?

MY FUTURE ISLAND

This 3D graphics project marked one of my initial ventures, sparked by the idea of modelling what my future island would look like. It featured elements such as a cozy wooden house, a towering lighthouse, a majestic palm tree, and, of course, my own parcels of land. I couldn't forget to include my cherished jet ski to complete the scene! It was my way of visually shaping my island dreams.

LUPUL NEWSLETTER

As part of the Graphic Design module in my Multimedia university degree, I was tasked with creating a newsletter for the opening of a new brewery in Barcelona, named LUPUL artbeer. This newsletter provided details about the opening party, highlighting the brewery's unique features, along with information on prices, activities, tasting sessions, and the courses on offer. It was an opportunity to blend design creativity with promoting an exciting new beer destination in the city.

MONTJUIC PHOTO EDITION

This project for the Photography and Video subject involved putting into practice image developing and retouching techniques using Adobe Photoshop software. Consequently, I decided to go to Montjuic, Barcelona, to capture an incredible photography to which I added my own touch of originality.

MP3 PLAYER WITH LED MATRIX

In this university project, we delved into the realm of Arduino, and for my final work, I chose to create an MP3 player. This player not only reproduced melodies from iconic songs like "Despacito" by Luis Fonsi or the "Pirates of the Caribbean" theme but also featured a captivating synchronization of LEDs to the music's rhythm, making the experience even more incredible. It was a blend of technology and creativity that truly made the project stand out.
